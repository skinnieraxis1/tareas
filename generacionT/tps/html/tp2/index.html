About Vault-Tec

Welcome to Vault-Tec Corporation, the premier provider of state-of-the-art underground vaults designed to safeguard humanity from the ravages of nuclear war and other cataclysmic events. Founded in the years leading up to the Great War, Vault-Tec is dedicated to preserving the human race and ensuring its survival in the face of adversity.

Our mission goes beyond mere construction; it encompasses a commitment to scientific inquiry and social experimentation. Vault-Tec's vaults serve as crucibles of human behavior, allowing us to study the effects of isolation, resource scarcity, and other factors on societal dynamics.

While our methods may sometimes be misunderstood, rest assured that every decision we make is driven by a desire to secure a brighter future for humanity. Vault-Tec: Building a Better Tomorrow, Today.

Vault-Tec's Desires

Vault-Tec's primary objective is the preservation of humanity and the continuation of the human species in the event of global catastrophe. Our vaults are meticulously designed and constructed to provide a safe haven for inhabitants, shielding them from the horrors of the outside world.

However, our goals extend beyond mere survival. Vault-Tec seeks to understand the intricacies of human behavior and society under extreme conditions. Through our social experiments conducted within the vaults, we aim to glean insights that will shape the future of civilization.

At the heart of Vault-Tec's desires lies a commitment to progress and enlightenment. While some may question our methods, we remain steadfast in our belief that the knowledge gained from our experiments will ultimately benefit humanity as a whole.

Vaults

Vault-Tec has constructed a network of vaults spanning the breadth of the United States, each one a testament to our dedication to the survival of humanity. From coast to coast, our vaults stand ready to shelter those fortunate enough to secure a place within their reinforced walls.

Within these vaults, residents will find not only safety and security but also the opportunity to participate in groundbreaking social experiments. From Vault 13's role as a control vault to Vault 112's immersive virtual reality simulation, each vault offers a unique glimpse into the human condition.

While the specifics of each vault's design and purpose may vary, all share a common goal: to ensure the continuation of the human species and the preservation of knowledge and culture for generations to come.
